## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is an AI agent?">
An AI agent is an intelligent system that can perform tasks, make decisions, and adapt its behavior based on context. In Arahi, agents are built with memory, tools, workflows, and natural language reasoning to automate real-world business processes — from handling support tickets to qualifying leads.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How customizable are Arahi AI agents?" >
Every Arahi AI agent is fully customizable. You can tailor their workflows, tools, memory, and behavior to match your unique operating procedures, no-code required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When should I use an agent instead of a tool?" >
Use tools when your workflow is fixed and predictable. Use agents when flexibility, decision-making, and natural interaction are important. Agents excel in dynamic environments where adaptability and context understanding are key.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can build agents on Arahi?" >
Anyone with domain expertise can build agents — no technical skills needed. Arahi is designed so business users, marketers, sales reps, and ops teams can create agents based on their real workflows using a visual builder.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
